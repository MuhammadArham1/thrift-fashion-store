WHY THRIFTED?


All our website items are hand selected for their quality and style. We only select the best vintage items, removing all torn or marked items from the system; any errors at all will be photographed and detailed in the description. We sell only authentic vintage pieces and original brands, no fakes ever.


WHY VINTAGE?


Buying vintage clothing is a sustainable fashion choice. By shopping vintage as an alternative to buying new clothing, we reduce the demand for new items and the environmental impact they create. By keeping vintage items in circulation we power the circular fashion economy, whilst lowering the number of fashion items sent to landfill.
